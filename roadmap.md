# Speak-First Road to Conversational Cantonese — for Iris

The goal is to **talk** before you read. So this plan front-loads the spoken
colloquial language (Jyutping + tones), uses characters only for the eye, and
saves reading for Phase 2. ~15–25 min/day; the progress file overrides this plan —
never rush past shaky tones.

## Phase 1 — SPEAKING

### Week 1 — survival
- Lock in the **6 tones** (drill them every day — they carry meaning).
- Greetings & politeness: 你好 / 早晨 / 唔該 / 多謝 / 對唔住 / 拜拜.
- 係 / 唔係, 有 / 冇, 好 / 唔好 — and yes-no questions (好唔好呀?).
- Say real things: "我係 Iris", "我有…", "我冇…".

### Week 2 — me & mine
- Pronouns + 嘅 (possessive): 我嘅, 你嘅, 佢嘅.
- Family words; introduce yourself and your family.
- **Classifiers I** (個/本/張/杯/隻): "一杯咖啡", "兩個朋友".
- Question words in place: 你叫乜嘢名? 你去邊度?

### Week 3 — daily life
- Food & drink, yum cha vocabulary.
- Order at a 茶餐廳: "唔該, 我想要…", "凍定熱?", "埋單!".
- Shopping & money: "幾多錢?", 貴/平, 買/賣.
- **Classifiers II** (碗/架/條/件) + counting what you eat/buy.

### Week 4 — getting around & time
- Time, days, 而家/今日/聽日/琴日; 點鐘 (o'clock).
- Places & directions: 屋企/公司/廁所, 左/右, 呢度/嗰度.
- Transport: 搭巴士/的士/地鐵; "去邊度?".
- **咗** for completed action: "我食咗飯".

### Week 5+ — converse
- Weather, feelings, small talk: "今日天氣點?", "你點呀?", "幾好".
- Plans & invitations: "一齊去飲茶, 得唔得?".
- Phone basics: "喂? 搵邊位?".
- Role-plays: ordering food, shopping, directions, meeting someone.

## Phase 2 — READING (switch on when speaking is solid)
- Start recognising the highest-frequency characters already in the app
  (你 我 佢 係 唔 有 冇 食 飲 去 好 大 細 …).
- Move from "say it" to "read it, then say it".
- Keep speaking as the anchor; reading sits on top.

## "Can-do" checklist (Phase 1 target)
- [ ] Greet, thank, apologise, say yes/no politely
- [ ] Introduce myself (name, where I'm from, a little about my family)
- [ ] Use numbers for price, age, time; pick the right classifier
- [ ] Order food and drink and ask for the bill
- [ ] Ask and answer simple questions (邊度 / 幾多錢 / 幾時 / 點)
- [ ] Talk about what I want / have / like (想 / 有 / 鍾意)
- [ ] Say what I did today using 咗
- [ ] Get around: ask directions, take transport
- [ ] Produce the 6 tones clearly enough to be understood
- [ ] Handle short, predictable everyday exchanges out loud
